Nov. 27 — Dec. 8, 2023

TOPO23

A Workshop on the Mathematics of Topological Order

At University of Tübingen, Germany

Topological phases of matter have garnered significant interest over the past 30 years, offering a unique blend of condensed matter physics, quantum field theory, algebraic topology and quantum information theory. While many of the key questions surrounding topological phases have a mathematical nature, the field still requires solid mathematical foundations. This workshop aims to address these challenges by offering a series of lectures on selected mathematical topics and presenting state-of-the-art results in topological phases.
